💬 Communication Best Practices
🧵
Always Thread
Reply to messages in thread to prevent clutter and keep conversations organized.
🔔
Never Mute Slack
Keep notifications on - important updates about your depo may come through at any time. If Depo Support responds to your request, you won't see it if notifications are muted.
✅
Close Your Tickets
When Depo Support helps you, close the ticket by wrapping up the thread.
🤝
Be Respectful
This is a shared workspace. Communicate professionally.
🏷️
Use Appropriate Channels
Post questions and updates in the relevant channel - don't clutter unrelated spaces.
📝
Be Clear & Concise
Provide all needed information upfront - job numbers, specifics, what you've tried.
